Our Solution

Nanocellulose-based packaging formats for clinical workflows

Platform

Sustainable Medical Packaging Platform

A platform of nanocellulose-based packaging formats designed to replace conventional plastic across critical clinical workflows — from device packaging to secondary supply chain materials.

Applications

Key Applications

Medical Device Packaging Surgical Kit Packaging Procedure Tray Packaging Healthcare Supply Chain Secondary Medical Packaging

Impact

Sustainability Impact

NHS Net Zero Objectives

Direct contribution to national healthcare decarbonisation goals.

Reduction of Healthcare Plastic Waste

Lower volumes of persistent plastic entering the clinical waste stream.

Lower Clinical Waste Disposal Burden

Reduced incineration cost and emissions per procedure.

Sustainable Procurement Compliance

Supports PPN 06/21 and NHS sustainable supplier requirements.

Reduced Carbon Impact

Lower embodied and end-of-life emissions across the packaging lifecycle.

Circular Healthcare Material Innovation

Materials designed for renewable inputs and end-of-life return.

Market

Target Markets

  • NHS Trusts and procurement consortia
  • Medical device manufacturers
  • Surgical kit and tray assemblers
  • Healthcare distributors and supply chain partners
  • Private hospital groups and clinical providers

Services

Consultancy & Partnerships

During our product development phase, EcoNanoMed offers consultancy on sustainable packaging strategy, material substitution assessments, and NHS Net Zero alignment for medical device and healthcare supply partners. We welcome collaborations with academic, clinical and industrial stakeholders advancing biomaterials for healthcare.
