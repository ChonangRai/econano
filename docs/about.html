About EcoNanoMed

Transforming healthcare sustainability through innovative biomaterials

Our Mission

Founded to Address Healthcare's Biggest Sustainability Challenge

Founded by Suren Ghale and Anil Ghale to address one of the most urgent challenges in modern healthcare — the growing environmental and financial burden of single-use medical plastic waste.

EcoNanoMed aims to transform healthcare packaging by developing biodegradable, high-performance nanocellulose-based materials suitable for medical and healthcare applications. We believe that innovation in sustainable materials is not just environmentally necessary — it's clinically and commercially essential.

Challenge

The Problem with Medical Packaging

The NHS generates an enormous volume of single-use medical plastic waste each year. Most sterile medical packaging is petroleum-derived, difficult to recycle, and costly to dispose of — placing growing pressure on healthcare budgets and sustainability targets.

Plastic-heavy

The majority of sterile packaging relies on multi-layer plastics and laminates.

Hard to recycle

Contaminated or mixed-material packaging is rarely accepted by recycling streams.

Costly disposal

Clinical waste incineration drives significant operating and carbon costs.

Misaligned with Net Zero

Current packaging works against NHS sustainability and procurement commitments.

Approach
