Material

Why Nanocellulose?

Nanocellulose is a renewable, plant-derived biomaterial with mechanical and barrier properties suited to demanding medical applications. It offers a credible, biodegradable pathway away from petroleum-based packaging plastics.

  • High strength-to-weight performance
  • Biodegradability
  • Renewable sourcing
  • Strong oxygen barrier potential
  • Compatibility with coating technologies
  • Reduced dependence on petroleum-based plastics
